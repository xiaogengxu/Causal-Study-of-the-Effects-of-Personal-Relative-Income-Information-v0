The questionnaire data, including the rewards, charitable donations and lottery data collected on this page, will be sent to Statistics Finland. The officials (who are specialized experts at collecting and storing data on Finnish individuals, families, firms and organizations) will extract the rewards, donations, and lottery data only from the questionnaire data. They will then connect those pieces of data with your contact data (using the code in the top right corner of your questionnaire the key of which is only available to the SF officials) and send them to an external professional agent (Enkätsfabriken AB, not related to the research team and unaware any details of the study). No other information or data will be revealed to the external agent. The external agent then contacts you and sends you the money, lottery tickets and receipts for donations.
